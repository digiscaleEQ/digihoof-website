About DigiHoof

DigiHoof was created to address a persistent challenge in equine practice: the communication gap between veterinarians and farriers when it comes to hoof radiograph findings. Radiographs contain critical information about hoof balance and pathology, but that information is only useful when both professionals can see it clearly.

The platform generates standardized, shareable hoof radiograph reports with consistent overlays and measurements — giving veterinarians and farriers a common visual language for discussing hoof health and making trim or shoeing decisions together.

Built for Real-World Equine Practice

DigiHoof reflects the operational realities of busy equine clinics. Report generation is fast enough to fit into high-volume imaging days, because when creating reports is quick, it's far more likely to happen consistently.

Every design decision focuses on practical usability: rapid landmark placement, automated measurements, consistent layouts across visits, and mobile-friendly sharing that farriers can access without technical barriers.

AI-Assisted, Veterinarian-Led

DigiHoof uses machine learning to assist with landmark detection and measurement standardization on hoof radiographs. This helps normalize how reports are generated across cases while preserving the veterinarian's clinical judgment and final interpretation.

The goal is not to replace expertise but to make professional-quality reporting realistic within the time constraints of everyday practice.

Better Collaboration for the Horse's Benefit

When veterinarians and farriers work from the same clear data, the horse benefits. DigiHoof ensures that hoof balance, alignment, and change over time are communicated consistently — supporting better trimming, shoeing, and management decisions.

Longitudinal tracking with standardized reports makes real comparison possible across visits, turning hoof radiography into a collaborative, ongoing conversation rather than a static snapshot.

Operated by DigiScan LLC

DigiHoof is operated by DigiScan LLC, a United States–based company focused on developing modern equine imaging and reporting tools.

Built by an equine veterinarian who works directly with farriers, DigiHoof is grounded in real-world practice — not theory. The mission is to deliver professional reporting tools that actually get used because they fit into how practices work.
