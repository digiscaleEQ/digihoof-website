Privacy Policy

1. Overview

DigiHoof is a hoof radiograph reporting platform operated by a United States–licensed company. We are committed to protecting user data and maintaining responsible data practices.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect account information (name, email, clinic name), uploaded imaging data, report data, system logs, and technical information necessary to operate the platform securely.

3. Use of Data

Data is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve the DigiHoof platform, ensure system security, and provide user support.

4. Data Ownership

All uploaded imaging data and generated reports remain the property of the uploading clinic or user. DigiHoof does not claim ownership of clinical imaging content or reports.

5. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed and stored in secure cloud infrastructure. By using the platform, users consent to cross-border data processing. Users in the European Union and other regions may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data in accordance with applicable law.

6. Security Measures

We implement encryption in transit, access controls, and authentication safeguards designed to protect user data from unauthorized access.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us via our support form.
